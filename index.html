Film Outline Schedule

This is a current outline of rough dates for my 2nd year film

Development (September - October)

  1. Script

    Have a full locked outline with locked Dial.

  2. Character Designs

    Lock designs for all three characters.

  3. Vis Dev.

    Roughs of backgrounds / style / world.

Pre Production (November)

  1. Vocal Record

    Record all character Dial.

  2. Tie-down Boards

    push acting poses with the new dial. record.

Production (November - April)

  1. Backgrounds

    Backgrounds should be limited to as few as possible. EST, full room shot, only a couple angles for talking heads.

  2. Rough Animation

    Roguh out poses for the dial. no mouth shapes neccessary. Focus on the timing of the acting poses

  3. Full Animation / Tie Down

    Fill in inbetweens and mouth shapes.

Post Production

  1. Grading / Filtering

    Filter and color final animation

  2. Sound Design

    Record / pull all remaining audio from sound library
